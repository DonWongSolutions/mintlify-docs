---
title: "Setting up your students"
---

In this guide you will learn how to setup MyCluster accounts for your students.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Log into the MyCluster Teacher Dashboard">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login with your Google Account.

    <Note>
      If you haven't yet got a Teacher Account, please refer to the guide [here](/setting-up-your-account)
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Go to the Users Tab">
    On the Sidebar on your left, select "Users" or go to /admin/dashboard/users

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/UsersPage.png"
        alt="Image"
        title="Image"
        lightAlt="Image"
        darkAlt="Image"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click on the "Bulk Add Students" button'>
    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Column>
        <Frame>
          ![Image](/images/BulkAddStudentsModal.png)
        </Frame>
      </Column>
      <Column>
        The popup on the right will open, prompting you to enter:

        Your student's emails (**One Email Per Line)**

        Their assigned templates

        <Note>
          Refer to here for instructions on creating VM Templates.
        </Note>

        Click "Create Accounts" when finished.
      </Column>
    </Columns>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for your students to accept their invitations">
    An invitation email will be sent to your students. They will need to accept the invitation before they can login to MyCluster. The email will look like the one below:

    <img
      src="/images/image.png"
      alt="Image"
      title="Image"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"62%" }}
    />

    <Tip>
      You can check which students have not yet accepted their invitation by viewing the "Lock Status" column on the User Management page.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
