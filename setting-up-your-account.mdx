## How to get a MyCluster Teacher Account

<Tip>
  Note: If you contact **Don Wong,** he will be able to set all these up for you.

  However, if Don has already left the school by the time you read this, you will have to do this yourself.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Creating a MyCluster Account">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and register a **student account**

    <Note>
      You will need to use your @[abingdon.org.uk](http://abingdon.org.uk) email to do so.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Locating the MyCluster Container">
    SSH into 20.238.53.135 and login with your own credentials

    <Note>
      _This is assuming that MyCluster is still being hosted on the Microsoft Server._
    </Note>

    Run `sudo docker ps -a `to identify the container running MyCluster

    Run the following command to set your student account associated with your email address as a teacher.

    `docker exec -d {container}  npx tsx tools/set-admin.ts --email {your_email}`

    <Note>
      _Remember to replace the variables above with real values._
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Login to MyCluster again">
    Go back to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login again with your Google Account. This time, you should be redirected to the Teacher Dashboard.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
