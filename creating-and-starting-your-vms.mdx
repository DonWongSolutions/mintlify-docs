---
title: "Creating and Starting your VMs"
---

In this guide you will learn about creating your own and accessing your VMs.

### Before you start...

- Please make sure that you have an account on MyCluster and
- Make sure that your teacher has unlocked your account.

### Creating your VMs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Login to your student dashboard">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login with your Abingdon Google Account. You will be redirected to the student dashboard after login.

    <Frame>
      ![Student Dashboard](/images/StudentDashboard.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Create your VM">
    Press the "Create My VM" button to create your VM.

    <Warning>
      Please only press the button** once**. Refresh the page if the VM doesn't show up after 30 seconds.
    </Warning>

    You will see an interface like the one below after your VM has been created.

    <Frame>
      ![Student Dashboard With VM](/images/StudentDashboardWithVM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Starting and Accessing your VMs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1:  Login to your student dashboard">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login with your Abingdon Google Account. You will be redirected to the student dashboard after login. You should see a VM has already been created. If not, follow the steps above to create your VM.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Step 2: Press the "Start" button to start your VM'>
    Once your VM starts running, it may take up to 3 minutes to boot up.

    **Please keep refreshing the page until the IP address field is populated.**

    <Frame>
      ![VM With IP](/images/VMWithIP.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title='Step 3: Press the "Connect (RDP)" button to connect to your VM'>
    You will be able to access your VM after accepting the AUP.

    <Frame>
      ![Remote Connection Student](/images/RemoteConnectionStudent.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
