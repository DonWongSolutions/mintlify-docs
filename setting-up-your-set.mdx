title: "Setting up your set"
---

In this guide you will learn how to setup your set.

### How to Create a new set and assign VMs into it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Log into the MyCluster Teacher Dashboard">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login with your Google Account.

    <Note>
      If you haven't yet got a Teacher Account, please refer to the guide [here](/setting-up-your-account)
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Go to the Sets page">
    On the Sidebar on your left, select "Sets" or go to /admin/dashboard/sets

    <Frame>
      ![Sets Page](/images/SetsPage.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Create your set">
    Create your set by pressing the "Create New Set" button on the top right corner.

    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Column>
        <Frame>
          ![Create New Set Modal](/images/CreateNewSetModal.png)
        </Frame>
      </Column>
      <Column>
        Fill out your **Set Name**

        The **Yeargroup** dropdown should populate automatically, but if not, adjust manually.
      </Column>
    </Columns>

    <Note>
      If you can't see your newly created set, please refresh the page.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Assign VMs to your set">
    <Tip>
      It is recommened to do this on your first lesson when your students just created their VMs
    </Tip>

    Go back to the** Overview** Page or /admin/dasboard

    You can now select multiple VMs and use the** Assign Set** button to assign the VMs into your newly created set.

    <Frame>
      ![Overview Page](/images/OverviewPage.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to power on all VMs in a set and unlock accounts associated with them:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Go to the Sets page">
    On the Sidebar on your left, select "Sets" or go to /admin/dashboard/sets

    This time you should see your set populated with the assigned VMs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Startup all of your student's VMs">
    You can do this by pressing the **"Start"** button

    Wait approximately 30 seconds and press the **"Unlock Set"** button to allow your students to login and connect with their VMs
  </Step>
</Steps>
