---
title: "Accepting your MyCluster Invitation"
---

In this guide you will learn how to accept your MyCluster Invitation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Locate your invitation email">
    You should receive an email like the one below when your teacher invites you to create an account on MyCluster

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/StudentInvitationExample.png"
        alt="Student Invitation Example"
        title="Student Invitation Example"
        className="mx-auto"
        style={{ width:"70%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Accept the invitation and sign up on MyCluster">
    Click on the **"Accept Invitation"** Button

    <Frame>
      ![Confirm Signup](/images/ConfirmSignup.png)
    </Frame>

    You will be redirected to this page where you have to either **login with your Abingdon Google Account (Recommended)** or **Set a Password**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: You're in!">
    After you have accepted your invitation, you should be logged into MyCluster Automatically.

    <Frame>
      ![Session Locked](/images/SessionLocked.png)
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
      You will not be able to create/access your VMs until your teacher unlocks your account.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
