---
title: "Adding a Passkey"
---

In this guide, you will learn about how to add a passkey to secure your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Log into your MyCluster Account">
    Go to [https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk](https://mycluster.abingdoncs.org.uk) and login to your account.

    <Frame>
      ![Student Dashboard With VM](/images/StudentDashboardWithVM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Go to the settings page">
    Using the dropdown menu available by clicking on your user avatar on the top right corner, you can go into your user settings page.

    <Frame>
      ![User Account Settings Page](/images/UserAccountSettingsPage.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Add a Passkey">
    Navigate to the "Security" tab, here you can add a passkey using the "Add a passkey" button.

    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Column>
        <Frame>
          ![Add Passkey Email Verification](/images/AddPasskeyEmailVerification.png)
        </Frame>
      </Column>
      <Column>
        Email Verification may be needed before you can add a passkey to your account.

        Follow any on screen instructions to save your passkey on your device.
      </Column>
    </Columns>
  </Step>
</Steps>
